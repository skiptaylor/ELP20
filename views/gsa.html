GSA Contract Information:

A GSA schedule is a federally awarded, long-term contract that lists the prices the federal government has agreed to pay for a vendor's commercial products and services. GSA schedules are the favored purchasing mechanism for most federal buyers. CTC has two GSA schedules:


GSA Schedule 736

Temporary and Administrative Professional Staffing (TAPS)
Contract Number: GS-07F-9674S

GSA Schedule 874

Mission Oriented Business Integrated Services (MOBIS)
Contract Number: GS-02F-001CA
