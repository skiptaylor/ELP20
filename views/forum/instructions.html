The Forum section is an active message board community available to all eduCONNECT™ representative.

There are 5 different areas of the Forum available for you to share and learn from each other.

Use the "Best Practices" forum to share productivity enhancing strategies and techniques that you have discovered, and that you think your peers could benefit from knowing.
Use the "Success Stories" forum to share significant customer achievements. Bragging on your efforts is encouraged and can be inspirational to others!
Use the three "Questions" areas to ask for ideas and solutions to any issues you may be having - and please share any possible solutions you may have for other posted questions.
