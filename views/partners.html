Partner Organizations

Burruss Institute for Public Service

The A.L. Burruss Institute of Public Service & Research is affiliated with Kennesaw State University in Georgia. Their mission is to enhance the ability of governmental agencies and non-profit organizations to make informed decisions for the public good by providing relevant data, technical resources, and skill development. CTC has partnered with Burruss on initiatives including teen driving safety, leadership training for the GA Department of Juvenile Justice, and on studies related to public education.

National Ability Center

The National Ability Center in Park City, Utah provides recreational experiences for special needs clients from age 3 to age 75. Their motto is consistent with CTC’s philosophy of self challenge and motivation: “If I can do this, I can do anything.”


Summit Consultants

This service-disabled, veteran owned small business provides certified professional facilitation services and facilitation training. CTC has successfully partnered with this company to provide supplemental facilitation services to our customers, and we have even used their facilitators on numerous occasions. If you need a professional facilitator, we highly recommend!

Rivers of the World

Rivers of the World is a not-for-profit Christian organization that serves remote areas of the world. ROW meets with the indigenous people, discovers their problems, and makes them our problems. Jimmy Shafe is the Director of the ROW Congo-Kasai Chapter.


Université Protestante au Congo

Université Protestante au Congo is a university (theology, law, business, medicine) in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of the Congo. Jimmy Shafe serves on the North American Liaison Bureau Board.
