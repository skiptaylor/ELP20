Corporate Team


Jimmy Shafe

President/Owner
Started company in 1973

Jimmy's primary responsibilities include overall guidance and direction of the company; creation and delivery of custom training packages; marketing and project management of corporate initiatives; and instruction and speaking at numerous training events and conferences. Some of his accomplishments include: leading the project team in the creation of Career Direction, an outreach package that has been presented to over 6 million high school students nationwide; leading the team in creation of the HEAR anti-bullying workshops in collaboration with professors from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and the Harvard Graduate School of Education; and primary creator of the TEAM leadership training initiative for more than 200,000 Army National Guard leaders.

Amy Smith

Educational Programs Consultant
Joined CTC in 2012

Amy originally joined CTC in 2012 as an educational liaison for the state of Georgia. After a year's experience in that role, she then became CTC's Director of Educational Programs in the fall of 2013. Amy earned a Ph.D. in Educational Psychology and has enjoyed teaching, consulting, managing research projects, studying abroad and writing. With a particular interest in helping students develop positive self-concepts, motivation, and knowledge and skills needed for successful futures, Amy encourages educators and members of the sponsoring organization, the Army National Guard, to effectively take advantage of CTC's educational resources. Amy currently serves as a consultant to CTC.
